Near vs Far — The Core Idea

Distance from the speaker → the farther the bar, the farther the object
singular
THIS
Close — next to you · "this phone" (in your hand)
THAT
Far — over there · "that car" (across the street)
plural
THESE
Close — next to you · "these keys" (in your hand)
THOSE
Far — over there · "those buildings" (in the distance)

01

Each Word in Detail

THIS
Singular + Near
1 thing · close to you · present moment
👇
This is my phone.
Este es mi teléfono. (lo tengo en la mano)
Can you try this coffee?
¿Puedes probar este café? (lo tienes enfrente)
This is Ana — she's my friend.
Esta es Ana — es mi amiga. (presentando)
I love this song!
¡Me encanta esta canción! (sonando ahora)
This week is very busy.
Esta semana está muy ocupada. (tiempo presente)
THAT
Singular + Far
1 thing · away from you · past moment
👉
What is that building over there?
¿Qué es ese edificio de allá?
Did you see that?!
¿Viste eso? (algo que pasó)
I loved that movie we watched.
Me encantó esa película que vimos. (pasado)
Can you pass me that book on the shelf?
¿Me pasas ese libro del estante?
That was a great idea!
¡Esa fue una gran idea! (ya pasó)
THESE
Plural + Near
2+ things · close to you
🤲
These are my new shoes!
¡Estos son mis zapatos nuevos! (los llevo puestos)
Have you tried these arepas?
¿Has probado estas arepas? (están en la mesa)
These photos are amazing!
¡Estas fotos son increíbles! (las estás viendo)
I bought these at the market.
Compré estos en el mercado. (los tienes)
These days everyone uses their phone.
Hoy en día todos usan el teléfono. (tiempo presente)
THOSE
Plural + Far
2+ things · away from you · past
🔭
Look at those mountains!
¡Mira esas montañas! (a lo lejos)
Who are those people over there?
¿Quiénes son esas personas de allá?
Those were the best years of my life.
Esos fueron los mejores años de mi vida. (pasado)
I remember those old songs.
Recuerdo esas canciones viejas.
Those shoes on the top shelf — can you get them?
Esos zapatos en el estante de arriba — ¿puedes alcanzarlos?
02

Adjective vs Pronoun — Two Ways to Use Them

These words can work in two ways: before a noun (adjective) or alone (pronoun). Same word, two roles!

✦ As an Adjective — before a noun
This book is great.
Este libro es genial.
this + noun ✓
That restaurant was delicious.
Ese restaurante estaba delicioso.
that + noun ✓
These mangoes are fresh.
Estos mangos están frescos.
these + noun ✓
Those old photos are beautiful.
Esas fotos viejas son hermosas.
those + noun ✓
I want this color, not that one.
Quiero este color, no ese.
this/that + noun ✓
◈ As a Pronoun — replaces the noun
This is delicious! (= this food)
¡Esto está delicioso!
this alone = pronoun ✓
Is that your bag?
¿Esa es tu bolsa?
that alone = pronoun ✓
These are mine.
Estos son míos.
these alone = pronoun ✓
Those look expensive.
Esos parecen caros.
those alone = pronoun ✓
Hello, this is María. (on the phone)
Hola, soy / habla María. (por teléfono)
phone intro — classic use! ✓

Special Uses — Beyond Just Distance

📞
Phone Calls

⏰
Time Reference

esta semana / año / mañana

🤝
Introductions

04

Example Sentences

◈ THIS — singular near
This is the best empanada I've ever eaten!
¡Esta es la mejor empanada que he comido!
◈ THAT — singular far
What is that tall building over there?
¿Qué es ese edificio alto de allá?
◈ THESE — plural near
These are the documents you need to sign.
Estos son los documentos que necesitas firmar.
◈ THOSE — plural far
Who are those people dancing at the back?
¿Quiénes son esas personas bailando al fondo?
◈ THIS — time (present)
I'm really busy this week. Can we meet next Monday?
Estoy muy ocupado esta semana. ¿Podemos vernos el próximo lunes?
◈ THOSE — time (past)
Those were amazing times. I miss those days.
Esos fueron tiempos increíbles. Extraño esos días.
◈ ALL FOUR together
This bag is mine, that one is yours. These shoes are new, those are old.
Esta bolsa es mía, esa es tuya. Estos zapatos son nuevos, esos son viejos.
◈ THAT — reaction / comment
That was incredible! I can't believe that happened!
¡Eso fue increíble! ¡No puedo creer que eso pasó!

📌 Key Notes

  • THIS = singular + cerca de ti → "this phone" (singular) / "this is great" (pronoun)
  • THAT = singular + lejos de ti → "that car" / también para reacciones: "that was amazing!"
  • THESE = plural + cerca de ti → "these shoes" / "these are mine"
  • THOSE = plural + lejos de ti → "those mountains" / pasado: "those were great times"
  • Como adjetivo: va antes del sustantivo → this book · that idea · these people · those days
  • Como pronombre: va solo, reemplaza al sustantivo → This is mine. That looks good. These are new.
  • Por teléfono: This is Carlos speaking. = Habla Carlos / Soy Carlos
  • Para presentar personas: This is my friend Ana. (siempre THIS, nunca "here is")
  • Tiempo presente → this week / this year / this morning · Tiempo pasado → those days / that year / that time

⚠ Common mistakes:

✗ These is my friend.    ✓ This is my friend. (una persona = singular)

✗ I like this shoes.    ✓ I like these shoes. (shoes = plural → these)

✗ Look at this mountains over there!    ✓ Look at those mountains! (far + plural)

✗ That were amazing days.    ✓ Those were amazing days. (days = plural → those)

✗ Here is my colleague, Mark.    ✓ This is my colleague, Mark.
