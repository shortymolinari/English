Dates — US 🇺🇸 vs UK 🇬🇧 Format

The biggest difference: Americans write Month/Day/Year, British write Day/Month/Year. The same written date can mean different things!

🇺🇸 American English
OrderMonth / Day / Year
WrittenMarch 5, 2025
Numeric03/05/2025
SpokenMarch fifth, twenty twenty-five
FormalMarch 5th, 2025
🇬🇧 British English
OrderDay / Month / Year
Written5 March 2025
Numeric05/03/2025
Spokenthe fifth of March, twenty twenty-five
Formal5th March 2025
⚠ Careful!   04/07/2025 means April 7th in the US, but July 4th in the UK! Always write the month as a word to avoid confusion: July 4, 2025.
08

How to Say & Write Dates

Spoken formula Month + ordinal (1st/2nd/3rd...) + year  →  "March fifth, twenty twenty-five"
📅 Saying the day
Today is March the fifth.
Written: March 5th / 5th March
Hoy es el cinco de marzo.
📅 Full date + year
She was born on July 14th, 1998.
Spoken: July fourteenth, nineteen ninety-eight
Ella nació el 14 de julio de 1998.
❓ Asking about dates
What date is it today?
What's the date?
→ It's the twentieth of June.
¿Qué fecha es hoy? → Es el veinte de junio.
❓ Asking about day
What day is it today?
→ It's Monday. / It's Wednesday.
¿Qué día es hoy? → Es lunes / miércoles.
🎂 Birthday
My birthday is on December 3rd.
Spoken: December the third
Mi cumpleaños es el 3 de diciembre.
📅 With "on" preposition
The meeting is on Friday, April 18th.
Spoken: on Friday, April eighteenth
La reunión es el viernes 18 de abril.

Real Conversation

Valentina y el agente de viajes Miguel planean un viaje. Dorado = números · Verde azulado = fechas

V
Valentina
Traveler, Medellín
↔
M
Miguel
Travel agent, Bogotá
V
Hi Miguel! I want to travel to New York. I'm thinking about March fifteenth. Is that possible?
¡Hola Miguel! Quiero viajar a Nueva York. Estoy pensando en el 15 de marzo. ¿Es posible?
M
Yes! I have flights available on March 15th. There are two options: one at 6am and one at 3pm. The first one costs eight hundred dollars and the second costs one thousand and fifty.
¡Sí! Tengo vuelos disponibles el 15 de marzo. Hay dos opciones: uno a las 6am y otro a las 3pm. El primero cuesta 800 dólares y el segundo 1.050.
V
I'll take the first one! How many days is the trip? I want to be back by March twenty-eighth.
¡Me llevo el primero! ¿Cuántos días es el viaje? Quiero regresar antes del 28 de marzo.
M
From the fifteenth to the twenty-eighth is thirteen nights. The hotel in Manhattan starts at one hundred and twenty dollars per night — so around one thousand five hundred and sixty in total for the hotel.
Del 15 al 28 son 13 noches. El hotel en Manhattan empieza en 120 dólares por noche — así que alrededor de 1.560 en total por el hotel.
V
That works! What's the hotel address? And what floor is the room on?
¡Eso funciona! ¿Cuál es la dirección del hotel? ¿Y en qué piso es la habitación?
M
It's at 350 Fifth Avenue, on the 12th floor. The room number is 1204. Check-in is on Saturday the fifteenth after 2pm.
Está en la Quinta Avenida 350, en el piso 12. El número de habitación es 1204. El check-in es el sábado 15 después de las 2pm.
V
Amazing! My birthday is actually March twenty-first — I'll be turning twenty-eight! 🎂
¡Increíble! De hecho mi cumpleaños es el 21 de marzo — ¡voy a cumplir 28 años!
M
Happy early birthday! 🎉 I'll send the full itinerary to your email. The booking reference is NY-2025-0315. Total cost: two thousand three hundred and fifty-five dollars. Is that okay?
¡Feliz cumpleaños anticipado! Enviaré el itinerario completo a tu correo. El código de reserva es NY-2025-0315. Costo total: 2.355 dólares. ¿Está bien?
V
Perfect! Can you confirm the return date? It should be Friday, March twenty-eighth at 11pm, right?
¡Perfecto! ¿Puedes confirmar la fecha de regreso? Debe ser el viernes 28 de marzo a las 11pm, ¿cierto?
M
Correct! Friday the twenty-eighth of March, departing at 11pm, arriving in Medellín on March twenty-ninth at 6:30am. Enjoy your trip! ✈️
¡Correcto! El viernes 28 de marzo, salida a las 11pm, llegando a Medellín el 29 de marzo a las 6:30am. ¡Disfruta tu viaje!

📌 Key Notes

  • Ordinals for dates: always use ordinal numbers when saying dates out loud → March 5th = "March the fifth" or "March fifth"
  • Preposition on before days and dates: on Monday · on March 5th · on the weekend
  • Preposition in before months and years: in March · in 2025 · in the morning
  • Years 2010+: say as pairs → 2025 = "twenty twenty-five" (not "two thousand and twenty-five")
  • ⚠ forty = 40, not "fourty" — the U disappears! (four → forty)
  • Large numbers: use commas every 3 digits → 1,000 · 1,000,000 · 1,000,000,000
  • US vs UK dates: always write the month as a word to avoid 04/07 confusion!

⚠ Common mistakes:

✗ fourty    ✓ forty

✗ nineteen-ninety five    ✓ nineteen ninety-five (hyphen only between tens and units)

✗ on March    ✓ in March (months use "in", not "on")

✗ in Monday    ✓ on Monday (days use "on")

✗ two thousands and twenty five    ✓ two thousand and twenty-five or twenty twenty-five
